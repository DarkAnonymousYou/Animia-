Understanding the Metrics

Our Machine Learning model evaluates five crucial data points to provide an accurate diagnostic prediction.

Hemoglobin

The protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen. Low levels are the primary indicator of anemia.

MCV

Mean Corpuscular Volume measures the average size of your red blood cells.

MCH

Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin represents the average amount of hemoglobin per red blood cell.

MCHC

The concentration of hemoglobin in a given volume of packed red blood cells.
